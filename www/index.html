Welcome to Floofer! Before we get started, you just need to answer a teeny bit of information about what kind of floof you're looking for here!

In settings, you'll have the chance to pick what kind of animal you want (including breed), how old it should be, whether you want a boy or a girl, and how far you're willing to travel. You can choose your location (we recommend this for the best accuracy!) or enter a zip code. Don't worry – you can change your settings at any time!

The only required field is the type of animal you're looking for. Other than that, be as broad or specific as you want! But keep in mind – the more filters you add, the less floofers you'll get in your matches.
