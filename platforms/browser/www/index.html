Welcome to Floofer! Before we get started, you just need to answer a teeny bit of information about what kind of floof you're looking for here!

In settings, you'll have the chance to pick what kind of animal you want (including breed), what size, how old it should be, and whether you want a boy or a girl. You can choose your location (we recommend this for the best accuracy!) or enter a zip code. Don't worry – you can change your settings at any time!

The only required fields are the type of animal you're looking for, and either a zip code or a location to start up the search. Other than that, be as broad or specific as you want! But keep in mind – the more filters you add, the less floofers you'll get in your matches.
